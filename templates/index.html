Welcome to Jarrett Welding

Jarrett Welding and Machine Inc., owned and operated by John and Cindy Carey, is one of the leading fab, machining, and welding shops serving Danville Virginia and surrounding areas. Our mission is to provide services on time with competitive pricing and excellent quality, while maintaining lasting relationships with our customers. We strive to meet the needs of a variety of customers, from farmers to commercial and construction companies, while seeking new clients daily.
